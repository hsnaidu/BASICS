1. STRINGS 
    -> DATATYPE - THEY STORE A SEQUENCE OF CHARACTER WITHIN " "
        -> "hello world" , 'hello world' , """hello world"""

2. ESCAPE SEQUENCE
    -> \n : Next Line

3. OPERATION OF STRINGS
    -> 1. CONCATINATION : Adding two strings 
        -> "hello" + "world" = "hello world"
    
    -> 2. LENGTH : len()
        -> len('hello') -> 5
